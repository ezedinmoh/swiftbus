Popular Questions

Most frequently asked questions by our customers

How do I book a bus ticket?

Book tickets easily through our website or mobile app in just a few steps.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Telebirr, CBE, Bank of Abyssinia, Dashen Bank, and major credit cards.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or modify bookings up to 3 hours before departure.

How early should I arrive at the bus station?

We recommend arriving at least 45 minutes before departure time.
