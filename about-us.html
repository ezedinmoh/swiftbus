About SwiftBus

Transforming bus travel across Ethiopia with innovation, comfort, and reliability

Our Journey

Founded in 2020, SwiftBus emerged from a simple idea: to make bus travel across Ethiopia more accessible, reliable, and comfortable. Our founders, experienced travelers and tech enthusiasts, recognized the challenges faced by millions of Ethiopians who rely on bus transportation.

What started as a small startup in Addis Ababa has now grown into Ethiopia's leading bus booking platform, connecting over 50 destinations across the country. We've partnered with 15+ trusted bus companies to bring you the best travel experience.
